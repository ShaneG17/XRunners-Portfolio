About XRunners

We are a robotics team based in Los Angeles. We are a group of 8th grade students with one common goal, to make the world a better place. With our knowladge in robotics we hope to also teach others this great skill for perseverance, work ethic, communication and more.

Into The Deep

Dive deeper into our robot! Our robot is a specimen based robot that has the abilitie of all tele-op scoring elements. We use an active intake that then transfers our samples into a bucket that is on two symetrical linear slides. For our speciment "claw" we have a servo that is attached to a pole that is then attached to a rubber gripping system that can swing around for easy auto and tele op!
